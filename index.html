Notice of the visualization results table:

* The first row is results of MPV car without noise. The MPV car is normal testing car for having similar structures with the training set.
The second row is results of Pickup car without noise. The Pickup car is hard testing car for having structures, like the isolated driver's cab and the open carriage, do not appear in the training set.
* The first column is raw BP images generated by back-projection algorithm using data from 3 observation tracks.
The second column is the predicted images of the proposed method.
The third column is comparison of ground truth images and predicted images. We overlap ground truth images in blue color with predicted images in orange color to evaluate the performance. The predicted images approximate the ground truth images more closely than the raw BP results.
The forth column is the relative absolute error between the ground truth images and predicted images in space domain. The error mainly locates at the surface of cars, which indicates the ability of suppressing invalid information of the proposed method.

Notice of the Dominant Scatterers Error Histograms table:

* means this column shows the relative absolute error calculated using coordinates of top 0.1% strongest points in ground truth images.
For each histogram in this table, testing car name can be recognized from titles. MazdaMPV means the normal MPV car and ToyotaTacoma means the hard Pickup car.
Orbits item in titles show the three observation orbits, whose data is used to generate raw BP images.
Range means this histogram calculates relative absolute error using coordinates of top percentage strongest points in ground truth image.
Horizontal axis is the relative absolute error. Value is closer to ground truth if its relative absolute error is close to zero.
Vertical axis is the percentage of points in different errors.
Orange marks the results of our method. Blue marks the results of raw BP.
